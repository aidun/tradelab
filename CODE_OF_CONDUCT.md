# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

TradeLab is maintained as a professional, respectful collaboration space. We expect contributors, maintainers, and community members to act with empathy, clarity, and professionalism.

## Expected behavior

Examples of behavior that help the project:

- giving constructive feedback
- assuming good intent while staying clear and direct
- discussing technical disagreements respectfully
- keeping communication focused on the work
- making the project more accessible to new contributors

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- repeated hostility or bad-faith engagement
- publishing private information without permission
- derailing technical collaboration with abusive conduct

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers may moderate discussions, close unproductive threads, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, report it privately through the support path in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).

## Enforcement

Maintainers will review reports and choose an appropriate response based on the severity and context of the situation.
